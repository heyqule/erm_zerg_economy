Version: 1.0.0
Date: 2025-04-??

  Features:
    - This mod is an internal library.  It does nothing by itself.
    - All recipes requires [space-age] mod to be enabled.
    - [space-age] Added Larva egg item to replace captive biter, 30 minutes spoil timer.  Each enemy race can have its colored egg.
    - [space-age] Added recipe to create more larva egg (Biochamber).
    - [space-age] Added recipe to convert larva egg to nutrients (Biochamber).
    - [space-age] Added recipe to convert larva egg to biter egg (Biochamber).    
    - [space-age] Added recipe to produce uranium-238(10), larva egg(1), plasma(1) >>> uranium-238(11) (via Plasma Assembling Machine).
    - [space-age] Added recipe to produce promethium science pack(1), larva egg(1), plasma(1) >>> promethium science pack(2) (via Plasma Assembling Machine).
    - Added Synthetic Hatchery for player to build zerg units.
    - Added Synthetic zerglings, hydralisk, mutalisk, guardian, infested terran and ultralisk as playable units. They are not repairable.
    - Added Synthetic zerg damage research to upgrade their damage (10 x 20%).
    - Synthetic zerg units support team color.
